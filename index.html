My first day at Redeemers University

Open Student Form

My first day in Redeemers was on a Sunday afternoon. I had left my home at Lekki Ajah in the morning and it took me a few hours to get to the school's location. Unfortunately my parents couldn't come with me, so I entered public transport by myself.

On getting to Akoda bus stop I entered a cab that went straight to my hostel, the Prophet Moses Extension Hall. I was checked in, signed, and given the key to my room. When I entered, the room was quite dirty. I prayed over the room and cleaned it up, arranged my things and slept off a bit.

I woke up in the evening to walk around and find where to get food. I bought Indomie and egg, went back to my hostel and ate while watching a movie. After which I lay on my bed and slept off.
